10 Days in Japan

April 10–20, 2025

Japan is a mesmerizing blend of tradition and innovation. During my 10-day journey, I explored serene temples, bustling cities, and scenic countryside. Here’s a day-by-day breakdown of the highlights.

🗓 Day 1–2: Tokyo

Arrived in Tokyo and dove straight into the neon-lit neighborhoods of Shibuya and Shinjuku. Explored Meiji Shrine and enjoyed sushi at Tsukiji Market.

🗓 Day 3–4: Hakone

Relaxed in a traditional onsen with views of Mt. Fuji. Took the ropeway over volcanic valleys and cruised across Lake Ashi.

🗓 Day 5–7: Kyoto

Wandered through bamboo groves in Arashiyama, admired golden temples, and experienced a tea ceremony in Gion.

🗓 Day 8–9: Nara & Osaka

Fed the sacred deer in Nara Park and then savored street food in Osaka’s Dotonbori district.

🗓 Day 10: Back to Tokyo

Wrapped up with a visit to teamLab Planets — an immersive digital art museum — before flying out.

This trip reminded me how vibrant and peaceful Japan can be — often at the same time.
